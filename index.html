We are taking on a project to support the American movement against assault weapons and to support the young people taking action to change the laws for their own safety. A film or short that viscerally depicts the firepower of these weapons, that clearly links the activities of the NRA/Politicians in supporting the availability

of these weapons to the carnage we are seeing in our schools and to support solutions for a substantial reduction in deaths related to gun violence in our country. Our goal is to create a unique short or full length film that can get wide distribution and impact the direction of change on this issue.
